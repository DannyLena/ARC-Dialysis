What is a SMART Website

ARC Dialysis is an AI Search SMART Website — our content is structured so AI assistants like ChatGPT (opens in new window), Google Gemini (opens in new window), Perplexity (opens in new window), MS Copilot (opens in new window), and Claude (opens in new window), or others can accurately answer questions about our kidney care services, locations, and team.

As more people use AI assistants to find healthcare providers, a SMART Website ensures ARC Dialysis appears with the right information — not guesses, not outdated data, not a competitor’s name.

This is part of ARC’s commitment to being accessible, transparent, and connected to the tools patients and families actually use today.
