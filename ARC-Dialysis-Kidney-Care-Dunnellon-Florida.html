Why Quality Dialysis Care Matters

When kidneys fail – whether due to long-term disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, or sudden injury – the body can no longer filter waste, control fluid balance, or regulate vital minerals effectively. Dialysis becomes a life-saving treatment.

With in-center hemodialysis, blood is filtered through a dialyzer (an "artificial kidney") to remove waste, extra fluids, and maintain electrolyte balance. This helps control blood pressure and supports vital bodily functions when kidneys no longer can.

For many people with advanced kidney disease, regular dialysis sessions (typically 3 to 4 hours, several times per week) offer a stable, reliable way to live with kidney failure while preserving as much quality of life as possible.

Choosing a high-quality dialysis center close to home means less travel, more time with family, and access to experienced professionals focused entirely on your well-being.

Your Care Journey at ARC Dialysis Dunnellon:

  1. Initial referral and evaluation – We work with your nephrologist to build a personalized care plan based on your medical history, lifestyle, and treatment goals.
  2. Regular dialysis treatments – Performed in-center with full medical supervision and a dedicated care team who knows you by name.
  3. Ongoing monitoring and support – Including fluid balance, blood chemistry, nutrition, and overall wellness tracking.
  4. Education and guidance – Helping patients and families understand dialysis, lifestyle adjustments, and long-term care planning.
  5. Coordination with local providers – We work closely with nephrologists and hospitals in Ocala, Inverness, and throughout the region.
