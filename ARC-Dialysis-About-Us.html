Meet our Executive Team

ARC Dialysis is South Florida’s largest independent, minority‑owned inpatient kidney care provider. We’re dedicated to elevating quality of life through proven expertise, compassionate service, and unwavering commitment to excellence. Our clinicians operate as one team with one purpose: to consistently exceed patient expectations while delivering superior clinical outcomes.

Meet the TEAM

Fred Dumenigo

CEO

Fred is the founder and a shareholder of AmeriCare Renal Center, Apollo Renal Center Miami, and Olympus Healthcare and currently serves as its CEO. He graduated with a Medical Doctor degree from Universidad Central del Este in San Pedro de Macoris, Dominican Republic in 1986.

Fred's introduction into the dialysis industry began in 1978 at age sixteen as a dialysis patient care technician and throughout the years he has been responsible for the opening and development of numerous de novo dialysis clinics as well successfully negotiating and establishing dozens of inpatient acute dialysis and skilled nursing facilities contracts throughout the State of Florida.

CARLOS J. GONZALEZ

Vice President of Operations
Outpatient Services

Carlos J. Gonzalez is the President of AmeriCare Renal Center, Apollo Renal Center Miami, and Olympus Healthcare now Virtus RenALL Solution Network, LLC. Mr. Gonzalez was accredited with a degree in Finance from Florida International University in 2008, and has earned his real estate license in the State of Florida.

Mr. Gonzalez began his career at an early age, finding much success in real estate ventures and was awarded the South Florida Chamber of Commerce Entrepreneur of the year award in 2013. Mr. Gonzalez founded a local Renal Facility, gathering a team of experienced Healthcare professionals to ensure quality dialysis treatments to all patients suffering from End Stage Renal Disease.

Mr. Gonzalez continues to hold a pivotal role in the strategic planning and success in Virtus, ARC, AmeriCare Renal Center, and Olympus business ventures.

Raul Lopez

VP Hospital Services

As the Vice President of Operations for ARC Dialysis Raul's main focus has always been on the patient. With a passion for the medical industry and renal care Raul strives to make a difference. Raul holds a degree in business as well as a Masters in Science of Nursing with over 20 years of dialysis experience.

In 2008 Raul was promoted to Director of Operations and spearheaded the restructuring of the Acute care program. After successful launches of University of Miami and Jackson Health system Raul was promoted to Vice President of Operations.

Christopher Gonzalez

Regional Director of Operations
Director of Purchasing & Logistics

Chris has been with the company since 2011. He started as a part time employee in materials management at Americare Renal Center, while finishing his Bachelors of Science at Florida International University. In 2015, he resumed his studies to acquire his Master's in Business Administration at Florida International University. Upon his graduation from his MBA he became the head of the department of purchasing and logistics. With his experience he has been able to negotiate great deals and pricing from vendors for the company.

In 2016, he took on a new role as an administrator for one of the company's clinics. In 2017, Chris also became the director of billing for the company. He was able to streamline the company's billing process, while at the same time cutting costs. In January 2021, Chris took on his latest role as Regional Operations Director.

Chris looks forward to the company's continued growth as well as his own professional development.

Nurian E. Robert MSN, MBA, HCM, RN, CNN

Director of Quality & Education

Nury, the Director of Quality and Education has many years of experience working in the healthcare industry. She began working in dialysis with one of ARC's founding fathers and throughout her lifetime she has worked towards improving the life of the renal failure patients. She earned her BSN from the University of Miami and went on to obtain an MSN and MBA focusing on Healthcare Management.

Throughout her career, Nury has made it her mission to educate others about the specialized care required by patients experiencing kidney disease. Her experience includes working with acute and chronic patients as well as working as a disease case manager all in the effort of improving the quality of care for renal patients. She is passionate about education and has created training programs for both healthcare providers and patients.

Nury is certified in nephrology nursing (CNN) and is a current member of the American Nephrology Association (ANNA) in which she has previously held officer positions in the local chapter.

Arlene Rivera

Supervisor

Arlene Rivera, RN, BSN, FNP, serves as Director of Operations for ARC Dialysis in the West Palm Beach region.

She has more than 20 years of nursing experience, including over 15 years in nephrology and acute dialysis care in hospital settings. She began her career as a bedside nurse and advanced into leadership, combining clinical knowledge with operational oversight.

In her role she manages daily dialysis operations across multiple hospitals, including staffing, regulatory compliance, quality improvement.

She works closely with physicians and clinical teams to maintain patient safety, strong documentation, and reliable dialysis delivery, while mentoring nurses and supporting professional development within the organization.

Maggie Freeman

Clinical Coordinator

Maggie Freeman, the Clinical Coordinator and Assistant DON has many years of experience working in dialysis and ICU settings.

She began working in dialysis in 1989 with one of the largest dialysis providers in the nation and joined the ARC team in 2017 as a Charge Nurse. She earned her BS from Barry University and her RN degree from Miami Dade College.

Throughout her career, Maggie has worked with acute, chronic and PD patients experiencing kidney disease to improve their quality of life and overall well-being. She is passionate about providing holistic care to each dialysis patient and being a preceptor to nurses and dialysis technicians. Maggie was awarded RN of the year in 2018.

Robert Winston

VP of Business Development & Strategic Partnerships

Bob Winston serves as Vice President of Business Development & Strategic Partnerships at ARC Dialysis. Since joining the organization, Bob has been instrumental in elevating ARC's brand presence and competitive positioning within Florida's dialysis industry.

His efforts established ARC Dialysis as a major supporter of the National Kidney Foundation of Florida, where CEO Fred Dumenigo now serves on the board. Bob leads partnership development, physician recruitment, and community engagement—connecting ARC's mission of compassionate kidney care with the hospitals, physicians, and communities across the state.

Whether you're a nephrologist seeking a medical directorship, a dialysis practice exploring partnership opportunities, or a health system in need of specialized management services—Bob is your dedicated point of contact. He works closely with physicians and providers to create customized solutions, from new center development and joint ventures to à la carte operational support.

Contact Bob at (954) 448-8482 or RxWinston@gmail.com to start the conversation.
