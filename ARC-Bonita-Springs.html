ARC Dialysis Services

We are dialysis providers

 

ARC Dialysis is an independent dialysis provider. We specialize in providing acute, outpatient and in-home therapies. 100% minority owned and Diversity Certified.

ARC Dialysis has a robust portfolio of Health Systems and Hospitals where we deliver inpatient dialysis services in seven different states. We also provide dialysis services in many skilled nursing and rehab facilities. The development of free-standing dialysis centers began several years ago to support our largest clients. ARC now operates twelve free standing centers, offering incenter dialysis, home and PD modalities. Our Mission

To become the provider of choice for patients, physicians, Hospital Systems, and payors to make a sound difference in the lives of patients challenged by Kidney Disease.

Our Pledge

Our trustworthy team is committed to providing the highest quality of care with the combined objectives of being results focused, flexible and adaptable, maintaining cost effectiveness, but always with active oversight to maintain program compliance with the highest integrity. Our leadership team has over 150 years of combined dialysis experience serving the kidney community.
