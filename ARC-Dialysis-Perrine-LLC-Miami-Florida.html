Compassionate Kidney Care on the South Dade Corridor

ARC Dialysis Perrine brings quality dialysis care to South Miami-Dade's growing communities along the US-1 corridor. Located on South Dixie Highway in the heart of Perrine, we serve families from Cutler Bay to Palmetto Bay, from Richmond Heights to Goulds—and everyone in between. As part of ARC Dialysis, Florida's largest kidney care network with 12 centers statewide, we combine the clinical resources of a major provider with the personal touch of a privately owned, minority-owned company that treats every patient like family.

South Miami-Dade is a community of hardworking families, longtime residents, and newcomers who've made this corner of Florida home. We understand that dialysis patients here often balance treatment with work, family responsibilities, and long commutes. That's why we've built a center focused on convenience, flexibility, and genuine care—so you can manage your kidney health without putting the rest of your life on hold.

Getting to ARC Dialysis Perrine

By Car

We're located directly on US-1 (South Dixie Highway) at 22165 South Dixie Hwy, making us easy to find from anywhere along the corridor. From the Florida Turnpike (Ronald Reagan Turnpike), take the SW 216th Street or SW 211th Street exit and head east toward US-1. From Old Cutler Road in Palmetto Bay, head west to US-1 and turn south. Free parking is available on-site with wheelchair-accessible spaces near the entrance.

By Metro Express BRT & Metrobus

Miami-Dade County's new Metro Express Bus Rapid Transit service runs along the South Dade TransitWay, connecting Florida City and Homestead to Dadeland South Metrorail station. While our center isn't directly on the TransitWay, several connecting bus routes serve the Perrine area. Route 52 travels through Richmond Heights and Perrine along the TransitWay corridor. Route 35 connects Cutler Bay to the TransitWay stations. From Dadeland South, you can connect to the entire Metrorail system for access throughout Miami-Dade County.

Paratransit & Ride Services

Miami-Dade Special Transportation Service (STS) provides door-to-door paratransit for eligible patients throughout the county, including South Dade communities. Many of our patients also use Uber, Lyft, or family transportation. Our location on US-1 makes pickup and drop-off straightforward for any driver. If you need help arranging transportation, our patient care coordinators can connect you with local resources.

Comprehensive Kidney Care Services

At ARC Dialysis Perrine, we provide the full spectrum of dialysis services to support patients at every stage of their kidney care journey:

  • In-Center Hemodialysis — Our comfortable treatment area features modern dialysis equipment, reclining chairs, and attentive care from our nursing team. Free WiFi and personal entertainment options help your three-to-four-hour sessions pass more comfortably.
  • Home Dialysis Training & Support — For patients interested in peritoneal dialysis or home hemodialysis, we offer comprehensive training and ongoing clinical support so you can treat in the comfort of your own home.
  • Hospital Coordination — We work closely with Baptist Health Homestead Hospital, South Miami Hospital, and HCA Florida Kendall Hospital to ensure smooth transitions between hospital stays and outpatient dialysis.
  • Vascular Access Management — Regular monitoring and coordination with vascular surgeons to keep your fistula or graft functioning optimally.
  • Nutritional Counseling — Guidance on managing your diet for kidney health, with practical advice that works with the foods your family already enjoys.
  • Social Services Support — Help navigating insurance, transportation, and community resources throughout South Miami-Dade County.

Why Your Dialysis Center Matters

When your kidneys can no longer filter waste and excess fluid effectively, hemodialysis takes over this vital function. For most patients, that means three sessions per week, three to four hours per session. Over the course of a year, you'll spend more than 400 hours in your dialysis center—that's why choosing the right one matters so much.

At ARC Dialysis Perrine, we've designed our center around the realities of life in South Miami-Dade. Our US-1 location means you're not fighting traffic through congested areas or navigating unfamiliar neighborhoods. Our bilingual staff ensures clear communication regardless of your preferred language. And our flexible scheduling helps you balance treatment with work, family, and everything else that makes up your life.

Who We Serve & What to Expect

We provide dialysis care for patients with chronic kidney disease (CKD) stages 4-5, end-stage renal disease (ESRD), acute kidney injury (AKI), and those awaiting or recovering from kidney transplant. Whether you're newly diagnosed or transferring from another facility, here's what your care journey looks like with us:

  1. Initial Consultation — We review your medical history, discuss your treatment options, and answer every question you and your family have. Bring your loved ones—we want everyone to understand what's ahead.
  2. Care Planning — Your nephrologist and our clinical team design a personalized treatment plan based on your kidney function, overall health, lifestyle, and goals.
  3. Access Preparation — If you need vascular access created, we coordinate with area surgeons to ensure your fistula, graft, or catheter is ready when you need it.
  4. First Treatment — Our nurses guide you through your first session step by step, explaining what you'll feel and monitoring your comfort throughout.
  5. Ongoing Care — Regular treatment sessions, monthly lab work, and continuous support to help you live your best life with kidney disease.
  6. Life Integration — We work with you to schedule treatments around your job, your family, and the things that matter most to you.
