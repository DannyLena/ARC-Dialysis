Florida's Independent Dialysis Provider — Bringing Life-Saving Care Closer to Home

When you need dialysis, your life shouldn't revolve around a treatment schedule that doesn't work for you. At ARC Dialysis, we believe you deserve choices — where you receive care, how you receive it, and when it fits your life.

As Florida's 100% minority-owned, Diversity Certified independent dialysis provider, we're not part of a faceless national chain. We're your neighbors, serving communities across Florida with the flexibility and personal attention that corporate giants simply can't match.

Comprehensive Kidney Care Services

Our services span the full spectrum of kidney care: hemodialysis, peritoneal & (HDD) home dialysis. What truly sets us apart is our commitment to your outcomes — reduced hospital readmissions, shorter stays when hospitalization is necessary, and dedicated support to help you transition smoothly between care settings.

You shouldn't have to choose between quality care and convenience. With ARC Dialysis, you get both. Ready to explore your options?
