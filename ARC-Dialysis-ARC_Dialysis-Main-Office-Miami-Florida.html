Leadership in Kidney Care Across Florida & Beyond

Welcome to the corporate headquarters of ARC Dialysis — an independent, 100% minority-owned, diversity-certified dialysis provider headquartered in Miami, Florida. From this office, we coordinate operations, strategic partnerships, business development, and quality oversight for one of South Florida's most trusted dialysis networks.

ARC Dialysis operates across three distinct service lines: hospital/acute dialysis services within major health systems, outpatient center-based dialysis at our network of free-standing centers, and comprehensive home dialysis programs including peritoneal dialysis and home hemodialysis. We are across all three modalities — one of the only providers in the region with this distinction.

Our leadership team brings over 150 years of combined dialysis experience to every partnership, every center, and every patient interaction. This depth of expertise, combined with our agile, independent structure, allows us to deliver solutions that larger organizations simply cannot match.

Note: This is our corporate administrative office — not a patient treatment facility. For dialysis services and patient care, please visit our Locations page to find the center nearest you.

Who We Partner With

Our corporate headquarters serves as the nexus for relationships with healthcare systems, hospitals, physicians, skilled nursing facilities, and business partners throughout Florida and across seven states where we deliver inpatient dialysis services.

Hospital Systems

We maintain partnerships with every major hospital system from Miami to West Palm Beach, providing inpatient dialysis services with dedicated teams and rapid response capabilities.

Nephrologists & Kidney Care Specialists

We are built around the nephrologist relationship. We offer medical directorship opportunities, collaborative outpatient and acute care models, joint ventures, and the kind of physician autonomy that large national chains simply cannot provide. Your clinical judgment drives care here — not a corporate protocol.

Skilled Nursing & Rehab Facilities

We deliver dialysis services to SNF and rehab patients, ensuring continuity of care during recovery and long-term stays.

Healthcare Investors & Developers

We partner on new center development, acquisitions, and management service agreements.

Medical Equipment Vendors & Industry Partners

Our headquarters is the point of contact for dialysis equipment manufacturers, technology vendors, supply chain partners, and industry support organizations. We evaluate new machines, home therapy devices, and clinical technology actively — and we move quickly when the right solution emerges. If you represent a product or service that improves dialysis care delivery, we want to hear from you.

Why Healthcare Partners Choose ARC Dialysis

In a market dominated by large national dialysis organizations operating thousands of centers, ARC Dialysis offers something different: the responsiveness of an independent provider with the capabilities of an established network.

Executive Accessibility

When challenges arise, large organizations send regional managers. We send our CEO. Fred Dumenigo personally attends major presentations, contract renewals, and problem-resolution meetings. This level of executive engagement is unmatched in our industry.

Speed of Innovation

Large providers take months to evaluate new technology and years to implement changes. We bring demonstrations to our headquarters immediately. We evaluate every new dialysis machine, every home therapy device, every AI platform that might improve care delivery. When innovation emerges, we move.

Physician Autonomy

National chains standardize everything because they cannot manage complexity across thousands of locations. We let physicians choose what works best for their patients. Our scale allows us to accommodate individual preferences while maintaining operational excellence.

Accountability Without Bureaucracy

With our network of centers, we cannot afford a single negative experience to go unaddressed. When issues arise, our physicians call stakeholders directly. Problems do not languish in ticket queues. We have relationships, not bureaucratic processes.

Our Track Record

Competitors sometimes tell hospital systems we are too small, that we cannot respond, that our operations might be problematic. Our results tell a different story:

  • We have converted skeptical chief nursing officers into advocates.
  • We pass all inspections without notations.
  • We accept difficult cases that other providers decline.
  • We build centers in underserved and rural communities where larger providers will not go.
  • We renew contracts with health systems that had every opportunity to switch.

Our reputation is built not on promises, but on consistent delivery.

Commitment to Access & Equity

ARC Dialysis was built on a simple belief: quality dialysis care should be available to everyone, not just those in profitable markets.

We serve communities that larger providers ignore. We build centers in rural areas with lower reimbursement rates. We accept unfunded patients and work to find them coverage. As a 100% minority-owned, diversity-certified business, we understand the importance of culturally sensitive care and community connection.

Our commitment to access is not a marketing message — it is woven into every decision we make about where to build, whom to serve, and how to deliver care.
