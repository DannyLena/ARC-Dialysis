Compassionate Kidney Care in Coconut Grove & Coral Gables

Americare Renal Center brings the resources of Florida's largest kidney care network to one of Miami's most vibrant and historic neighborhoods. Located on SW 37th Avenue near Mercy Hospital, we provide compassionate, high-quality dialysis services to patients in Coconut Grove, Coral Gables, Coral Way, Shenandoah, and surrounding South Miami communities. Whether you've called "The Grove" home for decades or recently moved to this charming bayfront area, our bilingual care team is ready to support your kidney health journey.

As part of ARC Dialysis — a privately owned, minority-owned provider with 12 centers across Florida — we deliver the personalized attention of a neighborhood clinic backed by the clinical expertise of a statewide network. Our center offers in-center hemodialysis, home dialysis training, and comprehensive kidney care coordination, all with the personal touch that sets us apart from large corporate chains.

Why Choosing the Right Dialysis Center Matters

When your kidneys can no longer filter waste and balance fluids on their own, dialysis becomes essential. But not all dialysis experiences are the same. In a community known for its quality of life and cultural richness, you deserve kidney care that matches that standard.

Hemodialysis sessions typically last 3 to 4 hours, three times per week. That's a significant commitment — and where you spend that time matters. Americare Renal Center offers comfortable treatment chairs, personal TVs, Wi-Fi, and a calm environment where you can rest, read, or catch up on your favorite shows. Our staff knows your name, remembers your preferences, and notices when something isn't right.

Being in Coconut Grove means you're never far from Biscayne Bay, the art galleries, or your favorite Cuban restaurant. We're here to help you maintain that vibrant Miami lifestyle, even while managing kidney disease.

Who We Serve

Americare Renal Center welcomes patients experiencing:

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)

Whether you're newly diagnosed or have been managing CKD for years, we provide ongoing care and education.

End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD)

When kidney function drops below 15%, dialysis becomes necessary. We're here to make that transition as smooth as possible.

Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)

If you've been hospitalized at Mercy, Baptist South Miami, or elsewhere for sudden kidney complications, we provide seamless follow-up care.

Pre- & Post-Transplant Support

Working toward a kidney transplant? We coordinate your dialysis care before and after surgery.

Patients Transferring from Other Centers

Moving to Miami or switching providers? We make the transition easy.

What to Expect When You Join Our Care Team

  1. Initial consultation and evaluation — We review your medical history, current medications, and lifestyle to build a personalized care plan.
  2. Coordination with your physicians — If you're coming from another facility or have specialists at area hospitals, we work directly with your care team.
  3. Vascular access assessment — If you need a fistula, graft, or catheter, we coordinate with vascular surgeons throughout South Florida.
  4. Your first treatment — Our team walks you through every step, answers all your questions, and makes sure you feel comfortable and informed.
  5. Ongoing monitoring — Regular lab work, blood pressure checks, and nutritional assessments keep your treatment on track.
  6. Continuous support — From social workers to dietitians to our nursing staff, you'll have a full team behind you.

Getting to Americare Renal Center

Our center is located at 2601 SW 37th Avenue, Suite 138 — on Douglas Road between Coral Way and US-1, in the heart of the Coconut Grove/Coral Gables medical corridor. Here's how to find us:

By Car

From US-1 (South Dixie Highway), turn west onto SW 27th Avenue or Bird Road (SW 40th Street), then head to SW 37th Avenue (Douglas Road). From Coral Gables, take Coral Way east or Bird Road east to Douglas Road. We're in the medical office building on the east side of SW 37th Avenue, with free parking available in our lot. Accessible spaces are located near the entrance. If you're coming from Brickell, head south on US-1 — we're about 10 minutes from the Mary Brickell Village area.

By Metrorail & Bus

The Douglas Road Metrorail station is just a short distance from our center, served by the Green and Orange lines. From the station, you can walk or catch a quick ride. Metrobus routes 37, 40, and 42 serve the Douglas Road corridor, connecting to Miami International Airport, South Miami, and points throughout the county. The Coral Gables Trolley also connects at Douglas Road station, providing free service to Miracle Mile and Merrick Park.

Need a Ride?

Many of our patients use Miami-Dade's Special Transportation Service (STS) for door-to-door paratransit. We're also an easy Uber or Lyft destination — just use our address or search for "Americare Renal Center." Family members are welcome to wait in our comfortable lobby, browse the nearby Shops at Merrick Park, or grab lunch at one of Coconut Grove's many restaurants while patients complete their treatment.

Coming from Coral Gables or Coconut Grove? Our center is ideally positioned between these two beloved neighborhoods. Whether you're walking from the Grove's shady streets or driving from the Gables' elegant boulevards, quality dialysis care is just minutes away. Call us to discuss the best route from your home.
